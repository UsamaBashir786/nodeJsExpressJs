About Us

We're passionate about building awesome web applications with modern technologies like Node.js, Express, and Bootstrap.

Our Vision

To become a leading example of how modern web technologies can be used to build performant, scalable applications with beautiful interfaces.

Our Story

Founded in 2023, My App began as a simple learning project to explore Node.js and Express. What started as a weekend experiment quickly grew into a full-fledged application as we discovered the power and flexibility of these technologies.

Today, we continue to evolve, incorporating the latest web development practices and frameworks to deliver the best possible experience for our users.
